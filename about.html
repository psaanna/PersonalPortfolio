About Me

I’m a passionate UX designer with a strong foundation in user-centered design, dedicated to creating engaging and intuitive digital experiences. My journey began with a Bachelor’s degree in Cognitive sciences, later I decided I wanted to work more with design and then started my journey in UX design, where I honed my skills in user research, prototyping, and visual design. Now I am keen to enhancing my skills in web development.

Skills

  • User Research: Conducting interviews and usability testing to understand user needs.
  • Prototyping: Proficient in Figma, and Adobe XD for wireframing and interactive prototypes.
  • Visual Design: Strong knowledge of design principles, color theory, and typography.
  • Collaboration: Effective communicator, skilled at working with cross-functional teams.
  • Problem-Solving: Creative approach to enhancing user experiences and addressing pain points.
