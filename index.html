About Me

I'm passionate about understanding how people think and interact, which led me to UX design. I love that UX keeps the focus on the user while balancing teamwork with developers and independent creative work. As a UX designer, I strive to create intuitive, accessible, and enjoyable digital experiences. For me, design isn’t just about aesthetics – it’s about solving real problems and enhancing user satisfaction. With over five years in sales, I’ve gained valuable insights into customer behavior, making my transition into UX design seamless. Both fields center around understanding people and creating solutions that meet their needs.
